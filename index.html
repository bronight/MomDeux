MomDeux
Every mother wishes there were two of her, one to run the household, and one to ... do everything else. Now there is, sort of, Mom Deux, the application that makes running the household that much easier, giving her time to revel in the joys of motherhood, enjoy that spa day, finish writing or reading that book, train for that marathon, etc., etc.

Calendar

Mom Deux makes the task of scheduling/calendaring of play dates, sports and/or dance and/or music practice, dental appointments, etc. a breeze, synching it all to your Google Calendar.

Chores

Mom (or Dad, can’t leave the Dads out) can assign chores, have Mom Deux send out reminders when they aren’t done by a certain time, and the assignees can ‘check off’ when the chore is done, allowing Mom Deux to notify Mom (or Dad) that the chore is done, making it all well and peaceful with the world again.

Locator

With just a quick check of Mom Deux, Mom (or Dad) can see where their family members are on the map. Hmmm, if he’s not home yet, maybe he can stop by the supermarket and pick up some milk, and a dozen eggs...

Shopping List

Hmmm, we need milk, eggs, gorilla glue... Honey, what else do we need to get?! A pack of 3 inch nails, 2 2 x 4s, and some blueberries for my smoothie! Uhmm... ok.
